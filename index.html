Biography

Hello, my name is David Gureya. I am Erasmus Mundus Joint Doctoral Fellow at Universidade De Lisboa, Instituto Superior Técnico, Portugal and KTH Royal Institute of Technology, Sweden. I work on "Resource Allocation for Data-Intensive Services in the Cloud" under the joint supervision of Prof. João Pedro Barreto and Prof. Vladimir Vlassov.
